Brand

Alise is an independent British lifestyle store, designing our own take on fashion and homeware.

Our story starts with our founder, Sam. Her creative upbringing, culturally influenced by the many countries he lived in, instilled a love of design and an exploratory spirit from an early age. At university, whilst studying Full Stack, Alice began to bring back gifts for friends from his travels abroad. She turned this into a small business and in 2022 the first shop opened.

Growing

Today, Alisia is growing across the UK and Ireland and is run by an amazing team who share Olly’s passion for bringing new ideas to life. We have fun and follow our company values: Work Hard, Play Hard & Be Kind.
